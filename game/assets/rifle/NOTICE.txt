MA5 rifle mesh + textures (body.png, display.png, compass.png, number-*.png)
and the vertex data in ../rifle-model-data.js are the Halo: Combat Evolved
first-person assault rifle asset, carried over from the FIRST STRIKE
non-commercial fan project (github.com/NotASithLord/first-strike), which
sourced them under Microsoft's Game Content Usage Rules for non-commercial
fan creations.

The character meshes and textures in ../characters/ and
../../characters-data.js (Halo 2 marine, female marine, and infection form;
Halo 3 flood combat forms, civilian and ODST hosts) were converted by
scripts/convert-h3-assets.mjs from H3EK source art carried in the
Blackandfan H3EK-Tags repository (github.com/Blackandfan/H3EK-Tags), under
the same Microsoft Game Content Usage Rules for non-commercial fan
creations.
